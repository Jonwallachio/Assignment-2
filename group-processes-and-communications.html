Group processes and communications plan

The project team will utilise the following communication cadence.

1. Weekly meetings. The group committed to catching up weekly for at least 1 hour, to discuss the project and plan the approach.

2. The team communicated regularly utilising Microsoft Teams, with several dedicated channels, devoted to different aspects of the project.

4. The team committed to regular milestones in meetings.

5. The team held several ad-hoc calls and meetings when required.
