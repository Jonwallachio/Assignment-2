Work Life Balance Application Project – Plans and Progress

Project Overview

The Work Life Balance application began as an attempt to imagine a product that could provide genuine, realistic support and help for busy working people.

As we approached this work, we began with an initial concept, with a small number of key user features, tailored to our target market.

We then engaged in a long process of refinement to arrive at a final list of user features, a minimum viable product with which to begin development and customer feedback, and a project plan to progress the application forward.

See below a timeline of events:

1. August 2021

a. Initial concept developed (Assignment 1). Prototype developed.

Website created. Assignment 1

1. September 2021

a. Group formed. Product further refined.

b. Features refined and additional user features added.

c. Project plan developed.

1. October 2021

a. Website for Assignment 2 developed and deployed (Assignment 2).

b. Group focused on next phase of product development (Assignments 3 and 5).

1. November 2021

a. Last phase of product development commenced (Assignments 3 and 5).

b. Story board and scripting prepared and submitted (Assignment 3).

c. Final website developed and deployed (Assignment 5).
